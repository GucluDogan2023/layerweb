Design & Renovations

Design & Renovations

Where Vision Meets Master Craftsmanship

At Layer Pro Painting, we believe that a truly beautiful space starts with a strong design line. For over 20 years, we have been transforming interiors into architectural masterpieces, blending technical expertise with an unmistakable high-end aesthetic.

Our Design & Renovations division is dedicated to homeowners who seek more than just a fresh coat of paint—they seek a complete reimagining of their living environment. Whether you are looking for a chic, modern update or a timeless, architectural overhaul, our team delivers results that are as functional as they are breathtaking.

Our Specialized Services

We specialize in full-scale remodeling and meticulous renovations that elevate the value and soul of your home.

  • Kitchen Remodeling: The heart of the home deserves a high-end touch. From custom shaker-style cabinetry to premium Calacatta stone surfaces, we create culinary spaces that define elegance.
  • Bathroom Renovations: Transform your bathroom into a private spa. We focus on sophisticated textures, high-end fixtures, and layouts that maximize both comfort and style.
  • Plan Remodeling & Spatial Design: Sometimes the existing layout doesn’t fit your lifestyle. We rethink and remodel floor plans to improve flow, light, and architectural impact.
  • Interior Architecture: Our 20+ years of experience allow us to look beyond the surface, ensuring every renovation is structurally sound and aesthetically superior.

A Collaborative Approach to Luxury

Great design is a dialogue. To ensure every project meets the highest standards of luxury and "chic" sophistication, we work in close partnership with our sister company, Ira Design & Interiors.

By combining the precision of Layer Pro Painting’s execution with the visionary concepts of Ira Design, we provide a seamless "concept-to-completion" experience.
